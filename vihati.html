VIHATI THEATRE CLUB

Incepted in 2015, "Vihiti is recognized as the official Theatre Club of GLA University. Theatre is an art, concerned with the action which is precisely planned to present a real-life event in a coherent and significant sense of drama. Vihiti is the right platform where students can exhibit their acting, scriptwriting and directorial skills to give wings to their creative streak.
